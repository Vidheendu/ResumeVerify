ATS Pro Tips

01

Use Standard Section Headings

Stick to common headings like "Experience", "Education", "Skills" — ATS systems are programmed to recognize these.

02

Avoid Tables & Graphics

Most ATS cannot read content inside tables, text boxes, or images. Use a clean, single-column layout.

03

Include Relevant Keywords

Mirror the exact keywords from the job description. Use both spelled-out terms and acronyms.

04

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "Led", "Developed", "Achieved", "Implemented".

05

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers and percentages: "Increased sales by 30%" is far more impactful than "Improved sales".

06

Save as PDF

PDF preserves your formatting across all systems. Avoid .docx unless specifically requested.
